PokeMatch

Welcome to the PokeMatch! A fun memory game that test your memory!

How to Play

  • Please Select one of the options below, we have 8 10, or 12 cards.
  • When you start, you will be shown all the card locations for a few seconds, and you must memorize them.
  • After those few seconds, the cards will flip and it is your job to match each pair.
  • Match all pairs before the timer runs out to win! Good Luck, and go match em all!!

*Disclaimer* You will only see Gen 1 pokemon here so sorry not sorry. *Disclaimer*
